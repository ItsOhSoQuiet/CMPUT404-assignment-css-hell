About Me

Matthew Kluk is a Computing Science student at the University of Alberta in his final year of studies. Throughout his studies and work experience, he has done a variety of projects, from Android applications to web applications. In addition, as both an English minor, a former student journalist and a person who had to write research reports for his IT job, he is an efficient technical writer, who can write documentation, manuals, reports, press releases, Knowledge Base articles, etc. Matthew Kluk is loyal and hardworking, and can work with any team there is.
